About Our Company

Escrow Healthcare is dedicated to empowering healthcare providers across the Middle East with reliable, data-driven revenue cycle and administrative solutions. From accurate medical billing and coding to denial management and reconciliation, we help clinics, hospitals, and specialty practices streamline operations, accelerate reimbursements, and maintain full compliance with regional regulatory standards. Our expert team combines deep industry knowledge with advanced technology to deliver services that free up your staff to focus on what matters most — exceptional patient care.
At Escrow Healthcare, we simplify complex billing and revenue processes so healthcare professionals can focus on patients, not paperwork. Serving clinics, hospitals, and specialty practices across the UAE and Middle East, we provide tailored medical billing, coding, and revenue cycle management services. Our goal is to optimize your reimbursements, reduce denials, and build a solid financial foundation for sustainable growth.
At Escrow, we help healthcare providers achieve financial clarity and operational excellence. Our tailored Revenue Cycle Management (RCM) solutions cover every aspect of medical billing, coding, denial management, and reconciliation — enabling hospitals, clinics, and specialty centers to focus on patient care while we handle the complexities of your revenue operations.
